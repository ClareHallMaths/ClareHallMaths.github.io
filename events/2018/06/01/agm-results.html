AGM Results

Jun 1, 2018

We are pleased to announce the results of the CHMA Annual General Meeting. A new committee for the 2018-2019 academic year was successfully voted into place, as follows:

  • Arran Fernandez (president)
  • Matteo Giordano (secretary)
  • Mario Cekic (treasurer)
  • Tanguy Grall (public relations officer)
  • Hamed Masaood (events officer)

Also some minor changes to the CHMA Constitution were approved. Updated Constitution can be accessed at the usual place.

The Clare Hall Mathematical Association (CHMA) is a graduate student society for Cambridge students with an interest in mathematical sciences. Based in the college of Clare Hall, but open to all graduate members of the university, the Association was founded in 2017 and organises trips, talks, and other events in each term of the academic year.
