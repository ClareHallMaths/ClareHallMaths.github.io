The Clare Hall Mathematical Association (CHMA) is a graduate student society for Cambridge students with an interest in mathematical sciences. Based in the college of Clare Hall, but open to all graduate members of the university, the Association was founded in 2017 and organises trips, talks, and other events in each term of the academic year.
